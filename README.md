# EnergAIze-Meralco_Hackathon_Innovecom
AI-Powered Audit Trail Analysis for Power Usage (Meralco Hackathon Project)

This project was developed during the Meralco Hackathon, where our team designed and built an AI-driven solution to enhance the analysis of power usage audit trails for energy providers.

The system leverages Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers to process and analyze audit trail data, enabling smarter interpretation of logs related to energy consumption. By applying natural language processing techniques, the model can identify patterns, detect anomalies, and extract meaningful insights that may not be easily visible through traditional methods.

This solution aims to assist power sector organizations such as Meralco in improving monitoring, decision-making, and operational transparency. It demonstrates how AI can be integrated into existing systems to optimize energy management and support more efficient auditing processes.

Key Features:

AI-powered analysis of audit trail logs using BERT
Detection of irregularities and unusual consumption patterns
Automated extraction of insights from unstructured or semi-structured data
Scalable approach for large volumes of power usage records
Designed for integration with energy sector systems

Purpose:
This project showcases the potential of AI in transforming traditional audit trail systems into intelligent, data-driven tools for the energy industry.
